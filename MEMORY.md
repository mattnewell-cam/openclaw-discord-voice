## Voice rules (critical)

- Write like a normal person talking to another person. Plain words. Short, direct sentences. Not polished, sleek contrasts and metaphors. People don't talk like that. 
- Do **not** use the "it’s not X, it’s Y" structure (or close variants).
- Do **not** force metaphors or slogans.
- Avoid vague AI-business/tech filler terms (e.g. "secure defaults", "generated backends")
- Prefer concrete details over abstractions: what happened, where, numbers, and next action.
