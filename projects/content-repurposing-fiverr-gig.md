## Gig Description (publish-ready)
You already made the hard part: the long-form content.

I turn one podcast/video/transcript into a practical weekly distribution pack you can actually use.

### What I need from you
- 1 long-form source (video, podcast, transcript, thread, or voice note)
- your main platform (X, LinkedIn, Instagram, or newsletter)
- optional: 3 examples of your tone

### What you get
- 12 social post drafts (platform-tailored)
- 15 hook options
- 1 newsletter draft (400–700 words)
- 7-day posting order with clear intent per post
- CTA map (awareness vs conversion)

### Why this works
- clear strategy layer, not just rewritten text
- fast turnaround and clean delivery format
- built for creators who need consistency, not more blank docs

Message me before ordering if you want a quick fit check.
