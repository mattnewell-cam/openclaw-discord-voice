# Outbound DM Template (Creators)

## Short DM (first touch)
hey {name} — saw your {podcast/video/thread} on {topic}. solid stuff.

quick one: if you want, i can turn one long piece into a full weekly content pack (10 posts + 5 hooks + 1 newsletter) in 48h.

happy to do a sample from your latest piece so you can judge quality before deciding.

## Follow-up DM (48h later)
yo {name}, bumping this in case it got buried.

if useful, send one recent video/podcast and i’ll show you a mini sample (2 posts + 1 hook) so you can see if the fit is right.

no pressure either way 👌

## Call-to-Action Variants
- “want me to do a sample from your latest upload?”
- “if i send a quick sample today, would you want to see it?”
- “open to testing this on one episode this week?”

## Personalization Fields
- {name}
- {content type} (podcast/video/newsletter)
- {topic they cover}
- {specific recent piece}
